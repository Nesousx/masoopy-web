What to do when you have a shell?


## Linux

`history`

`whoami`

`uname -a`

`cat /etc/issue`

`cat /etc/passwd`

`sudo -l`

`crontab -l`

`ls -lhtrR /etc/cron*`

`ls -lhtrR /home`

## Windows

`set`

`getuid`

`ipconfig /all`

`arp -a`

`netstat -ano`
