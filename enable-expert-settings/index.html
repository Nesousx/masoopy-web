How to enable Expert Settings in #XBMC / #Kodi

Navigate to System > Settings. From here notice the Basic settings button in the bottom left corner.

Navigate to it (yes, you can!) and just press your Return keyboard’s key. You’ll see that the button now reads Expert settings.

Job’s done!
